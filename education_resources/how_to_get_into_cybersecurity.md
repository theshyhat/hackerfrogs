## Sampling Different Aspects of Cybersecurity
Most cybersecurity training exercises (called CTFs or Capture the Flag) are grouped into one of several categories, the most common ones being the ones listed below. For each category, there are two associated YouTube links that demonstrate what challenges in this category may look like:
* Web Exploitation (Website Security)

[PicoCTF - Secrets Challenge](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zAD2PH22Y5M&list=PLzVrhECG8ZbLn2l6jbBVWEqsoo-oyxA7p&index=43)

* Digital Forensics (Examination of Computer Files)

[PicoCTF - Extensions Challenge](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VpYtMF3z0x4&list=PLzVrhECG8ZbLn2l6jbBVWEqsoo-oyxA7p&index=66)

[PicoCTF - Lookey Here Challenge](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=ZWU3sDrmR_k&list=PLzVrhECG8ZbLn2l6jbBVWEqsoo-oyxA7p&index=61)

## Learning Tech Fundamentals
Doing almost any technical role in cybersecurity requires a basic knowledge of technical topics and computer networking. The following YouTube playlists are recommended as a minimum requirement for beginning training in technical cybersecurity roles. Keep in mind that these are not the only sources for learning basic tech knowledge, but recommendations:

[Professor Messer - A+ Training Course - Part 1](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=bnEKY6UJfKc&list=PLG49S3nxzAnna96gzhJrzkii4hH_mgW4b&index=3)
