# Leap Year Rules
## Discovered Rules
* if you jump normally, you die (level 0)
* if you jump, but you don't go to the full height of your jump, you don't die (level 0)
* if you land on spikes, you die (level 1)
* if you fall more than 2 blocks in height, then you die (level 1)
* if you land on a person, they break your fall, and you don't die (level 4)
* if you walk on the same plane as spikes, you don't die (level 7)
* if you fall more than 5 blocks in height, then you bounce up 4 blocks in height (level 15)
* if you fall more than 7 blocks in height, then you can tunnel into dirt (level 15)
