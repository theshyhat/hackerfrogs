# Rules For Toki Tori 2+
* we can do two things:
  * we can chirp
    * there are different types of chirps
  * we can stomp
* when we chirp, the bird comes over to fly above us
* when we stomp, the frog croaks
* if we stomp on certain tiles (rock bridge tiles), the tiles collapse
* stomping can wake up crabs
* chirping will bring crabs towards you
* if you stomp next to a crab, it moves away from you
* stomping on bamboo makes it go down (if there's more than one bamboo, the other one goes up)
* about frogs:
  * if you chirp, then the frog will turn to face you
  * if a frog has eaten something:
    * if you stomp, it will blow a bubble that can envelop you and make you float a certain distance
   
* big birds:
  * if you go near a big bird, it will pick you up and drop you off at a nest
    * the big bird will pick up any creature that comes near, not just you
  * if you are in tall grass, then the big birds won't see you
* small blue crabs
  * they can relay your chirps so they will reach places you can't get to
  * they will move away from you if you stomp
* melodies
  * you can chirp in a certain order to activate special effects
  * the rewind melody resets your progress back to the last checkpoint
    * two short chirps, then two long chirps
  * the collectable melody points us towards where the collectables are
    * one long chirp, then three short chirps
