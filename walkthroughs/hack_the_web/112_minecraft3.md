# URL
https://hack.arrrg.de/challenge/112
# concept
* the challenge asks us to flip the correct switches to make the redstone circuit in the picture work
* web research is used to solve the challenge
# method of solve
* identify that there isn't enough power in the redstone circuit to light the lamp
* take a look at the Redstone mini guide tab below the challenge description
* the switches operate in the same manner as an AND logic gate
* flip switches 1,3,4,5, and 7 to activate the lamp and then click on the `Open answer` button that appears
